1. Introduction

SilverOakApps Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") operates the All About Money application and the allaboutmoney.app website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

When you sign up for our alpha waitlist, we collect your email address. When you use the app, we may collect your name, email address, and information about your investment accounts that you choose to connect.

Information collected automatically:

We may collect usage data such as pages visited, features used, device type, and browser information to improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party services that help us operate (e.g., email delivery, hosting, analytics), but only to the extent necessary to provide our service. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link in each email. To request data deletion, contact us at the address below.

7. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies for functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy
