All About Money Marketing Site
This repository hosts the marketing website for All About Money, an innovative investment management app designed to empower investors with comprehensive financial insights and real-time market data across a broad spectrum of assets. The website showcases the app's features, benefits, and user interface, providing potential users with a clear understanding of how All About Money can enhance their investment journey.

Features:
Informative Content: Detailed sections highlighting the app's global market coverage, automated portfolio syncing, and dynamic analysis tools.
Responsive Design: Optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing, ensuring a seamless user experience across devices.
User-Friendly Layout: Simple and intuitive design that emphasizes easy navigation and clear presentation of information.
Call to Action: Encourages users to download the app from the App Store or Google Play and start managing their investments more effectively.
